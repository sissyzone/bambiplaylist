Bambi Playlist Creator v1.0.2


Bambi Playlist Creator is a small project that I (Katie, Kat for short) created because I was frustrated that I had to go and find specific files from all over the place and then use some sketchy audio file splicer to patch them together. The worse thing was that I also had to use up TONS of cloud storage if I wanted to share my playlist with anyone and go through the sharing permission shenanigans as well. Bambi Playlist Creator solves All these issues with playlist sharing, custom and easy ordering!!

Instructions: Click the checkboxes to select the file you want in your playlist. Drag the GRAY PART of the box in the change order section, if you click and drag the link part it will cause errors (if you accidentally do this, just check and uncheck a checkbox and it should fix it). Click create AFTER ANY CHANGES! Click the play and pause buttons to play and pause the playlist. Copy the permalink and share it with your friends to enjoy the Bambi Sleep Experience together!
